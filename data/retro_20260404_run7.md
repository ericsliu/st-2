## Key Issue: 0% Energy Before Summer Camp

### What happened
- **Turn 32** (Early May): Rested (energy 0%, conserve triggered)
- **Turn 33** (Early May): Energy 52%, raced NHK Mile Cup G1 — placed 3rd
- **Turn 34** (Late May): Energy 25%, raced Tokyo Yushun (Derby) G1 — placed 1st
- **Turn 35** (Early Jun): Energy 0%, 2 turns before summer camp
- **User intervention:** Stopped bot, did summer camp training manually (turns 37-40)

### Root Cause
G1 races bypass the lookahead conservation system (auto_turn.py line 2744: `is_mandatory` exempts G1s). At Turn 34, lookahead said "conserve for summer_camp_1" but the Derby G1 overrode it. The bot raced at 25% energy, draining to 0%.

### Fix Needed
Extend lookahead window from 5 to 7+ turns, and/or remove G1 exemption when milestone is within 3 turns and energy is critically low. Noted in `memory/project_lookahead_window.md`.
