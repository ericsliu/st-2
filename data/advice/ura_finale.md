# URA Finale Scenario Advice

## Stat targets (final run)
- Speed 1100+, Stamina 800+, Power 700+ are competitive minimums.
- S-rank runs typically reach Speed 1400+, Stamina 1000+.

## URA race requirements
- You must win 3 URA Qualifying races before the URA Finals. Don't skip races in year 3.
- Fan requirements are modest in URA — focus on stat optimization over fan grinding.

## Training strategy
- Years 1-2: Bond building + Speed foundation. Don't neglect Stamina.
- Year 3 (turn 49+): Pure stat maximization. Every train counts.
- The URA Finals require winning 3 consecutive races — Stamina matters most here.

## Event-specific notes
- "Special training" events: always accept if energy > 40.
- Bond events with support cards: almost always choose the option that raises bond.
- Mood improvement choices: prefer these when mood is Bad or Terrible.
