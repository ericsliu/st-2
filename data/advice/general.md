# General Uma Musume Training Advice

## Bond Building
- Prioritize support card bond in the first 20-24 turns. Aim for 60+ bond on all speed cards before mid-game.
- Friendship is #2 priority in Junior/early Classic, after race points + megaphones.
- Max friendship before Classic Year summer camp.
- No Recreation/Go Out in Junior Year — Great mood not needed until Classic.

## Energy Management
- Never train below 20 energy. Rest or use Infirmary at 20-30 energy.
- After a rest, you usually recover ~50 energy. Plan accordingly.
- Mood affects energy costs — training in "Terrible" mood is almost never worth it.
- Never train in summer with >5% failure rate — use energy items or rest.

## Mood Management
- Maintain Good/Great mood at all times.
- Great mood is required from Classic Summer onward.
- Check conditions before using Go Out/Recreation.
- Conditions are detected via Full Stats OCR every turn; auto-cure from inventory.

## Stat Priorities
- Focus on 2-3 stats deeply rather than spreading evenly. Specialization wins races.
- Wit improves training efficiency — a modest Wit investment (200+) pays off long-term.
- Don't neglect Guts entirely — races below 1800m heavily penalize low Guts finishes.

## Training
- Rainbow training tiles are almost always the best choice regardless of stat type.
- Hint icons should be prioritized — unlocking skills from hints is very efficient.
- Director appearances on a tile are a significant bonus.
- In the last 10 turns, ignore bond building entirely and chase your weakest target stats.
- Must tap each training bubble to preview gains; scan all five before deciding.
- Tapping a RAISED (already previewed) tile CONFIRMS training — skip it during scan.

## Racing
- ALWAYS check distance aptitude before race entry.
- Always use View Results to skip race animation. But View Results is locked for first-time race types — must tap Race instead.
- Never use Try Again unless user says it's an Ace run.
- Max 3 consecutive races — negative effects possible after 3.
- Always pick choice 2 on 1st place victory events.

## Skill Purchasing
- Skills not in the priority list should never be purchased (default priority 0).
- Never buy unique/inherited skills — they scale with career wins and are worthless in parent runs.
- Save 800 SP for Complete Career skill buying.

## Item Usage
- Use manuals/scrolls/carrots immediately after purchase.
- Never buy Energy Drink MAX EX.
- Item-boosted training gains show as two additive rows in OCR — must sum both.
