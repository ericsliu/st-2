# General Uma Musume Training Advice

## Bond building
- Prioritize support card bond in the first 20-24 turns. Aim for 60+ bond on all speed cards before mid-game.
- Friend-type support cards should be visited (Go Out) when their event triggers to maximize bond fast.

## Energy management
- Never train below 20 energy. Rest or use Infirmary at 20-30 energy.
- After a rest, you usually recover ~50 energy. Plan accordingly.
- Mood affects energy costs — training in "Terrible" mood is almost never worth it.

## Stat priorities
- Focus on 2-3 stats deeply rather than spreading evenly. Specialization wins races.
- Wit (IQ) improves training efficiency — a modest Wit investment (200+) pays off long-term.
- Don't neglect Guts entirely — races below 1800m heavily penalize low Guts finishes.

## General tips
- Rainbow training tiles are almost always the best choice regardless of stat type.
- Hint icons should be prioritized — unlocking skills from hints is very efficient.
- Director appearances on a tile are a significant bonus — take that tile unless energy is critical.
- In the last 10 turns, ignore bond building entirely and chase your weakest target stats.
